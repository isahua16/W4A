Meet the dynamic duo behind Red Deer's most delicious frozen treats. As passionate foodies, Julie and James always dreamed of opening their own ice cream shop. With a commitment to using only the freshest, highest quality ingredients, they have quickly become the go-to destination for sweet tooths in Red Deer.

After years of experimenting and perfecting their recipes at home, they finally took the leap and opened their own ice cream shop in Red Deer. When they're not in the kitchen whipping up their latest creation, they can be found exploring the local farmer's market, seeking out the best local ingredients to bring back to their shop. Come try a scoop and taste the love that goes into every batch!
